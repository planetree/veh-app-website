Automatic parking location

No pin. No ritual. Your parking spot stays ready.

veh. is being designed to remember the parking location when a trip ends, at the moment people usually forget to open a map.

Why automatic matters

Manual saving only works when you remember it. In real life, parking often happens in the background: get out, lock the vehicle, keep moving.

01

Less handling: The app should help quietly instead of adding another step.

02

Better timing: The useful moment is right after the trip, not later when you are already searching.

03

Clear value: When you return, you need location, distance, and a route.

Designed for Android and iOS

The app is being developed for both platforms. Background permissions, battery use, and privacy are important boundaries.

That is why veh. is planned as a calm, focused app: it remembers a parking position and helps you get back.

Parking should end simply.
