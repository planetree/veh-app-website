How it works

A simple idea: drive, park, find your way back.

veh. is being developed to keep the last parking position ready and make the way back easy to understand.

The flow

01

Recognize the trip: The app interprets everyday movement signals on the phone without turning the website into a technical blueprint.

02

Keep the parking place: When a trip ends, the relevant position should be saved as the parking location.

03

Guide you back: Later, the app shows distance, last parking time, and a route back to the vehicle.

What matters

Automatic parking detection is only useful when it is reliable and efficient. Android and iOS have different rules for background activity, location permission, and notifications.

So veh. is being built with privacy, battery use, and clear platform rules in mind. The app should help without pushing itself into the foreground.
