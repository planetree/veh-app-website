Find my bike

Your bike should not become a search either.

veh. is not only about cars. The idea also fits bikes: park, move on, and find the place again later.

Common situations

In the city, a bike is often parked quickly: near a station, in a side street, at a new place, or after a longer ride.

Unknown streets

When you do not know the area well, a saved location can help.

Many bike stands

Stations, campuses, city centers, and events can make finding the exact place harder.

More than one vehicle

Over time, the app can better support different vehicle types and everyday habits.

Calm interaction

The value should be there without making you think about the app all the time.

An app for the way back

For bikes, location accuracy matters because a few meters can make a real difference. Privacy and battery use still need to stay sensible.

That is why veh. is being developed step by step, starting with a clear core: last location, distance, and a route back.

For cars, bikes, and everyday life.

The first version focuses on reliable vehicle finding.
