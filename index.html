01 — The product

Parking should be the end of the drive, not the start of a search.

veh. is designed around one simple moment: you arrive, you leave your car, and life continues. Your parking position is kept ready for when you return.

No routine to remember. No map pin to drop when your hands are full.

02 — Designed for real life

Only what helps.
Nothing that gets in the way.

A focused experience made for the everyday situations in which you need to find your vehicle — not another app demanding attention.

CORE FEATURE

Automatic parking detection

On-device AI and the sensors in your smartphone help the app recognise when a drive has ended and retain the parking position without asking you to do anything.

A direct way back

See your last parking position, the distance to it and a walking route when you need one.

Made for everyday movement

Designed around real routines: driving, parking, walking away and returning later.

Privacy in mind

Location is used to make the experience useful, not to turn your everyday movement into a product. The app is designed to work efficiently in the background, with battery use kept in mind.

03 — Why veh.

More than a saved pin.
Less to think about.

Some map apps let you save a parking place. This app is built around the moment itself.

Rather than asking you to remember one more step, it uses that understanding to keep the one detail you may need later.

Where is my location data stored?

The app is designed to keep parking data on your device. If any service needs to process data, that will be explained clearly before early access.

Which platforms will the app support?

It is in development for Android and iOS.

When will it be available?

Early access is opening soon. The first public release will follow after feedback from that phase.

Will it affect my battery?

It is being designed to work efficiently in the background and to stay quiet when it does not need to act.
