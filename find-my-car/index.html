Find my car

Where did I park my car?

That is the moment veh. is being built for: you come back and want to know where your car is, without turning the street into a search.

Why it happens

Parking is often not a conscious final step. You get out, carry things, talk to someone, look for the entrance, or move on to the next appointment. Later, the one small detail you need is missing: the last place you left the car.

Unfamiliar places

In a new city, a large car park, or a side street, everything can look similar later.

Everyday pressure

When you are in a hurry, opening a map and dropping a pin is easy to forget.

Long pauses

After a few hours, memory is often less precise than it felt at arrival.

How veh. should help

The app is designed to keep the parking position ready when a trip ends. Later, you should see distance, time since parking, and a simple route back.

The goal is not to expose every technical detail. The goal is a focused helper for one clear job: finding your vehicle again when you need it.

Arrive easily. Return easily.

veh. is in development for Android and iOS.
